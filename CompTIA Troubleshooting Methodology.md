# ☕ CompTIA Troubleshooting Methodology

> A structured approach to IT support that helps technicians identify, isolate, fix, and document issues efficiently.

---

## 📌 Overview

This repository includes a summary of the CompTIA troubleshooting methodology and how it applies to IT support work. The goal of this process is to reduce guesswork, improve accuracy, and resolve problems in a repeatable way.

---

## 🛠️ Why It Matters

A troubleshooting methodology gives IT professionals a clear path to follow when something breaks. Instead of making random changes, technicians collect facts, test likely causes, apply the right fix, and confirm the system is working properly.

---

## 🔎 The 6-Step Process

### 1. Identify the problem
Gather symptoms, error messages, user reports, and recent changes. The more accurate the initial information, the easier it is to isolate the issue.

### 2. Establish a theory of probable cause
Use the evidence to form a likely explanation for the problem. Start with the most obvious and common causes first.

### 3. Test the theory to determine the cause
Check whether the theory is correct before making any changes. If the theory is wrong, revise it and test again.

### 4. Establish a plan of action
Once the cause is known, decide on the safest and most effective fix. Consider the risk, scope, and any business impact before moving forward.

### 5. Implement the solution and verify functionality
Apply the repair, then confirm the problem is actually resolved. Make sure the system works normally and that the fix did not create new issues.

### 6. Document findings, actions, and outcomes
Record the issue, the fix, and any lessons learned. Good documentation helps speed up future troubleshooting and supports team knowledge.

## 💡 Key Benefits

- ⚡ Faster issue resolution.
- 🎯 More accurate diagnosis.
- 🔁 Less trial-and-error troubleshooting.
- 🧠 Better consistency across support tasks.
- 📝 Stronger documentation for future reference.
